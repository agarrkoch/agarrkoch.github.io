Hi. My name is Aida Garrido.
I am a playwright.

I like to wield dialogue with violent efficiency, and to comic effect.

I've developed my work with The Tank, Romanian Cultural Institute, and The Drama League. I am a 2026 semi-finalist for Annie Baker's Cherry Lane Playwrights Collective.

I take my cues from Preston Sturges, Billy Wilder, Ernst Lubitsch, and Sophie Treadwell.

I am an archivist, with a focus on audiovisual preservation.

I believe in the importance of memory work, and I am passionate about open media and open standards. I never take for granted the legibility of digital objects and our access to them.

I have worked/consulted for CUNY TV, WGBH, Dia Art Foundation, New York Public Radio, David Zwirner, Blank Forms, EYE Filmmuseum, Rietveld Academie, and Brooklyn Museum.

I have presented my work and held workshops at the No Time to Wait (2025, 2023) and Association of Moving Image Archivists (2024) conferences.
MA Film Preservation, University of Amsterdam. BA History, Princeton University.
